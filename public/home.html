Rules to Mark Customer Risk Rating

# HIGH RISK MEDIUM RISK LOW RISK
1 If there are more than 10 incoming or outgoing (INN | OUT) transaction in a month from high risk country If there more than 6 incoming or outgoing (INN | OUT) transaction in a month from high risk/Non-high country If there less than 10 incoming or outgoing (INN | OUT) transaction in a month from non-high-risk country
2 If sum of all incoming (INN) transaction is more than 1000$ in a month. If sum of all incoming (INN) transaction is more than 600$ and less than 1000$ in a month. If sum of all incoming (INN) transaction is less than 600$ in a month.
3 If sum of all outgoing (OUT) transaction is more than 800$ in a month. If sum of all outgoing (OUT) transaction is ore than 500$ and less than 800$ in a month. if sum of all outgoing (OUT) transaction is less than 500$ in a month.
4 If number of transaction in a day is more than 20. If number of transaction in a day is more than 10 but less than 20. If number of transaction in a day is less than 10.
